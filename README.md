# Daneel

> Your AI digital twin and executive assistant—an intelligent extension ... of yourself.

## Overview

Daneel is an AI-powered digital twin and executive assistant that serves as a personal intelligence amplifier for leaders and professionals. It learns your communication style, understands your goals and context, and acts as a seamless extension of your capabilities—handling tasks, providing insights, and augmenting your decision-making.

## Features

- **Personalized Digital Twin** - Learns your communication style, preferences, and working patterns
- **Executive Task Management** - Handles scheduling, email drafting, research, and administrative workflows
- **Strategic Intelligence** - Provides context-aware insights and analysis for complex decisions
- **Continuous Learning** - Adapts to your evolving needs and maintains relevant context over time
- **Seamless Integration** - Works across your tools and platforms as a unified intelligence layer
- **Privacy-First** - Your data, your digital twin, under your control

## Philosophy

Daneel operates as an extension of you, not a replacement. Like its namesake, it's designed to be a trusted, always-available partner that understands your context deeply and acts in alignment with your goals. It's not about automation—it's about amplification.
